Pelicano Privacy Notice

Pelicano is designed to store clipboard history primarily on the user's local device.
During normal use, settings, history data, image cache files, and logs are stored in the
local application data folder of the current Windows user account.

Application behavior
1. Text, image, and file-drop history may be stored locally on the user's PC.
2. When automatic updates are enabled, the app may access the network to check version information
   and download installer files.
3. The app does not provide remote account creation or cloud sync as a default feature.

Website hosting and file delivery
The hosting or storage provider used for downloads or updates may process minimal technical access information
for service operation and security maintenance.
That information may include general request metadata such as IP address, access time, request URL, and user agent.
The developer may not separately collect or continuously review that information.

Users remain responsible for deciding whether to store clipboard history that may contain sensitive information.
Depending on personal or organizational policy, the feature may be disabled or the app may be avoided entirely.
